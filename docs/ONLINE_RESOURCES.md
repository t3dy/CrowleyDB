# The 40 Best Online Resources for Aleister Crowley and O.T.O. / A.'.A.'. Studies

This document catalogs the premier digital resources, archives, and scholarly platforms for researching Aleister Crowley, Thelema, and ceremonial magick. It is categorized to assist both the academic researcher (Lane C/E) and the magical practitioner (Lane A/D).

## I. Primary Texts, Archives, & The Libri

1. **Keep Silence (keepsilence.org/the-equinox)**
   * *Description*: The absolute gold standard for high-resolution, unedited scans of the original *Equinox* volumes and Crowley's early publications. Essential for checking primary formatting and original typesetting.
2. **Hermetic Library (hermetic.com/crowley)**
   * *Description*: One of the oldest and most comprehensive HTML archives of Crowley's works, Libri, and associated texts. 
3. **Thelema.org (The A.'.A.'.)**
   * *Description*: Maintained by the Jane Wolfe / Phyllis Seckler lineage (College of Thelema). Contains excellent reading lists, curriculum structures, and primary texts of the A.'.A.'.
4. **O.T.O. Grand Lodge Libraries (oto-usa.org / oto.org)**
   * *Description*: The official repositories for Ordo Templi Orientis history, manifestos, and legal standing of the copyrights.
5. **100th Monkey Press (100thmonkeypress.com)**
   * *Description*: An incredible bibliographical resource detailing the exact publication history, physical dimensions, and print runs of Crowley's first editions.
6. **Sacred-Texts.com (sacred-texts.com/oto)**
   * *Description*: A classic web 1.0 repository of the core O.T.O. and Crowley Libri.
7. **The Libri of Aleister Crowley (libri.oto-usa.org)**
   * *Description*: The official O.T.O. USA hosted versions of the Libri, generally reflecting the most current editorial corrections by Hymenaeus Beta.
8. **Crowley Collection at the Harry Ransom Center (hrc.utexas.edu)**
   * *Description*: Finding aids for one of the largest physical archives of Crowley's diaries, letters, and manuscripts at UT Austin.
9. **Warburg Institute Archives (warburg.sas.ac.uk)**
   * *Description*: Holds the Gerald Yorke collection, one of the most important collections of Crowley's magical diaries and correspondence. (Finding aids and some digital collections).

## II. Scholarly Research & Historical Analysis

10. **LAShTAL.com**
    * *Description*: The premier forum and news site for Crowley studies. The forums contain decades of debate among top scholars and practitioners.
11. **The Journal of Thelemic Studies**
    * *Description*: An independent, peer-reviewed (or academically rigorous) journal dedicated to the study of Thelema and Crowley.
12. **Center for History of Hermetic Philosophy (amsterdamhermetica.nl)**
    * *Description*: While broader than Crowley, this Amsterdam academic center (associated with Wouter Hanegraaff and Marco Pasi) publishes the most rigorous academic treatments of Crowley.
13. **Richard Kaczynski’s Website (richard-kaczynski.com)**
    * *Description*: The personal site of Crowley's definitive biographer, containing essays, errata for *Perdurabo*, and historical deep-dives.
14. **Tobias Churton's Publications**
    * *Description*: Updates and resources from the author of several granular Crowley biographies (e.g., *Aleister Crowley in India*, *The Beast in Berlin*).
15. **CESNUR (cesnur.org)**
    * *Description*: The Center for Studies on New Religions; offers sociological and historical papers on the O.T.O., Thelema, and their development as new religious movements.
16. **The Teitan Press**
    * *Description*: A publishing house (often associated with Martin Starr) that focuses on extremely rare, scholarly editions of Crowley's correspondence and historical documents. (Catalog is a great research tool).

## III. Qabalah, Tarot, & Gematria Tools

17. **Thelemic Gematria Calculator (various online tools)**
    * *Description*: Essential tools for calculating Hebrew and Greek isopsephy specifically tuned to Thelemic numbers (93, 156, 418, 666).
18. **The Book of Thoth Explorer (e.g., Tarotpedia / Tarot.com)**
    * *Description*: Sites that offer high-res breakdowns of Frieda Harris's paintings alongside Crowley's astrological and Qabalistic correspondences.
19. **Liber 777 Interactive**
    * *Description*: Various github-hosted web-apps that digitize the columns of *Liber 777* for cross-referencing pantheons and colors.
20. **Isis Lodge / A.'.A.'. Curriculum Guides**
    * *Description*: Online breakdowns of the specific tasks, reading lists, and memorization requirements for the grades of the A.'.A.'.

## IV. Notable Lineages & Practitioner Commentary

21. **The Temple of Silver Star (totss.org)**
    * *Description*: Academic and practitioner resources from an A.'.A.'. lineage, frequently publishing high-quality essays on Qabalah and the Holy Books.
22. **The Typhonian Order (Kenneth Grant)**
    * *Description*: Resources relating to the Typhonian tradition, which heavily interprets Crowley through a Lovecraftian, extraterrestrial, and left-hand path lens. (Starfire Publishing).
23. **Thelema Lodge Calendar (Historical Archive)**
    * *Description*: A massive archive of the newsletter run by Bill Heidrick, containing priceless historical essays, document transcriptions, and Qabalistic analysis from the 1980s-2000s.
24. **Lon Milo DuQuette's Archives/Patreon**
    * *Description*: The U.S. Deputy Grand Master of the O.T.O., offering the most accessible and practical explanations of Crowley's Tarot, Enochian magick, and Goetia.
25. **The Open Source Order of the Golden Dawn (OSOGD)**
    * *Description*: While GD-focused, their integration of Thelemic concepts and open-source ritual publishing offers great insights into the mechanics of the rituals Crowley adapted.

## V. Specific Workings & Mysticism

26. **Enochian Magick Reference (e.g., Benjamin Rowe's works)**
    * *Description*: Crucial for understanding Crowley's *The Vision and the Voice* (Liber 418) and his Enochian explorations in the desert.
27. **The Paris Working Resources**
    * *Description*: Online analyses of Crowley and Victor Neuburg's homosexual sex-magick operations invoking Jupiter and Mercury.
28. **Abbey of Thelema (Cefalù) Historical Sites**
    * *Description*: Photography archives and historical restorations of the Abbey's Chamber of Nightmares.

## VI. Audio, Video, & Cultural Reception

29. **Speech in the Silence (Podcast)**
    * *Description*: The premier Thelemic podcast featuring readings of the Holy Books, lectures on magick, and O.T.O. news.
30. **Living Thelema (Dr. David Shoemaker)**
    * *Description*: A podcast and resource site focused on the practical application of the A.'.A.'. system by a clinical psychologist.
31. **The Aleister Crowley Society (Facebook/Social)**
    * *Description*: Heavily moderated spaces where high-level historical document sharing and translation work occurs.
32. **Crowley on Wax (Original Audio)**
    * *Description*: Archives containing the digitized wax cylinder recordings of Crowley reciting the Enochian Calls and "The Call of the First or Second Aethyr."
33. **The Occult Humanities Conference (NYU / Phantasmaphile)**
    * *Description*: Archives of academic lectures covering Crowley's influence on art, music, and counter-culture.
34. **Jimmy Page & Boleskine House**
    * *Description*: Historical resources detailing the Led Zeppelin guitarist's acquisition of Crowley's Loch Ness estate and its recent restoration foundation.
35. **Jack Parsons & The Agape Lodge (JPL Archives / Biographies)**
    * *Description*: Resources detailing Crowley's American disciples in California, the Babalon Working with L. Ron Hubbard, and the intersection of magick and rocketry.

## VII. Specialized Document Repositories

36. **Invisible Basilica of Sabazius**
    * *Description*: Essays and historical documents curated by Sabazius X°, the National Grand Master General of the U.S. O.T.O.
37. **Agape (O.T.O. Newsletter Archive)**
    * *Description*: The official organ of the US Grand Lodge, containing decades of organizational history and essays.
38. **The Scarlet Letter**
    * *Description*: Various regional O.T.O. body publications that serialize historical Crowley letters and diaries.
39. **Crowley Marginalia Projects**
    * *Description*: Practitioner-led efforts to scan and transcribe the notes Crowley wrote in the margins of his personal books (e.g., his copy of *The Book of the Law* or *The Goetia*).
40. **Boleskine House Foundation (boleskinehouse.org)**
    * *Description*: The charitable foundation working to restore Crowley's former estate, offering historical context and architectural history of the site central to *The Book of the Sacred Magic of Abramelin the Mage*.
