# Online Resources for Crowley Studies

This document gathers digital resources for researching Aleister Crowley, Thelema, ceremonial magic, and the wider reception history around both. The list is organized for readers moving between primary texts, scholarship, and later practitioner interpretation.

## I. Primary Texts, Archives, and the Libri

1. **Keep Silence (keepsilence.org/the-equinox)**
   * *Description*: High-resolution scans of the original *Equinox* volumes and related Crowley publications. Best for layout, typography, and facsimile confirmation.
2. **Hermetic Library (hermetic.com/crowley)**
   * *Description*: One of the oldest and broadest HTML archives of Crowley's works, Libri, and associated texts.
3. **Thelema.org (A.'.A.'. curriculum)**
   * *Description*: Curriculum material, reading lists, and primary texts associated with the A.'.A.'. tradition.
4. **O.T.O. Grand Lodge Libraries (oto-usa.org / oto.org)**
   * *Description*: Official repositories for Ordo Templi Orientis history, manifestos, and copyright-related material.
5. **100th Monkey Press (100thmonkeypress.com)**
   * *Description*: Bibliographical notes on publication history, first editions, physical dimensions, and print runs.
6. **Sacred-Texts.com (sacred-texts.com/oto)**
   * *Description*: A classic web repository of core O.T.O. and Crowley texts.
7. **The Libri of Aleister Crowley (libri.oto-usa.org)**
   * *Description*: Official O.T.O. USA versions of the Libri, often with later editorial corrections.
8. **Crowley Collection at the Harry Ransom Center (hrc.utexas.edu)**
   * *Description*: Finding aids for one of the largest physical archives of Crowley's diaries, letters, and manuscripts.
9. **Warburg Institute Archives (warburg.sas.ac.uk)**
   * *Description*: Home to the Gerald Yorke collection, a major archive for Crowley's magical diaries and correspondence.

## II. Scholarship and Historical Analysis

10. **LAShTAL.com**
    * *Description*: A long-running forum and news site for Crowley studies. Useful for tracing debate, interpretation, and community history.
11. **The Journal of Thelemic Studies**
    * *Description*: An independent journal devoted to the study of Thelema and Crowley.
12. **Center for History of Hermetic Philosophy (amsterdamhermetica.nl)**
    * *Description*: An academic center whose publications often place Crowley in broader histories of Western esotericism.
13. **Richard Kaczynski's Website (richard-kaczynski.com)**
    * *Description*: Essays, errata, and historical notes from one of Crowley's major biographers.
14. **Tobias Churton's Publications**
    * *Description*: Articles and resources from a biographer whose work often emphasizes art, sex, and historical context.
15. **CESNUR (cesnur.org)**
    * *Description*: Papers and reports on new religious movements, including Thelema and O.T.O. history.
16. **The Teitan Press**
    * *Description*: A publisher of rare and scholarly editions of Crowley's correspondence and historical documents.

## III. Qabalah, Tarot, and Gematria Tools

17. **Thelemic Gematria Calculators**
    * *Description*: Tools for Hebrew and Greek isopsephy tuned to Thelemic number work such as 93, 156, 418, and 666.
18. **The Book of Thoth Explorer**
    * *Description*: Sites that present Frieda Harris's paintings with Crowley's astrological and Qabalistic correspondences.
19. **Liber 777 Interactive**
    * *Description*: Web apps that digitize *Liber 777* for correspondence browsing.
20. **A.'.A.'. Curriculum Guides**
    * *Description*: Online summaries of the tasks, reading lists, and memorization requirements associated with the grades.

## IV. Lineages and Practitioner Commentary

21. **The Temple of Silver Star (totss.org)**
    * *Description*: Practitioner resources and essays on Qabalah, ritual, and the Holy Books.
22. **The Typhonian Order / Kenneth Grant**
    * *Description*: Resources for the post-Crowley Typhonian current and its highly idiosyncratic interpretations.
23. **Thelema Lodge Calendar Archive**
    * *Description*: Newsletter archive with historical essays, document transcriptions, and Qabalistic commentary.
24. **Lon Milo DuQuette's Archives**
    * *Description*: Accessible practitioner-oriented explanations of tarot, Enochian magic, and Goetia.
25. **Open Source Order of the Golden Dawn (OSOGD)**
    * *Description*: A practical open-source ritual community with useful material on the mechanics of Golden Dawn and Thelemic practice.

## V. Workings and Mysticism

26. **Enochian Magic Reference Materials**
    * *Description*: Reference points for understanding Crowley's Enochian work and *The Vision and the Voice*.
27. **Paris Working Resources**
    * *Description*: Analyses of Crowley and Victor Neuburg's sexual-magical operations in Paris.
28. **Abbey of Thelema Historical Sites**
    * *Description*: Photography archives, restorations, and site histories related to the Abbey at Cefalu.

## VI. Audio, Video, and Cultural Reception

29. **Speech in the Silence**
    * *Description*: A long-running Thelemic podcast with readings, lectures, and community news.
30. **Living Thelema (Dr. David Shoemaker)**
    * *Description*: Practitioner-focused commentary on the A.'.A.'. system and practical Thelema.
31. **The Aleister Crowley Society**
    * *Description*: Moderated social spaces for document sharing, translation, and discussion.
32. **Crowley on Wax**
    * *Description*: Digitized audio recordings of Crowley reciting calls and rituals.
33. **The Occult Humanities Conference**
    * *Description*: Academic talks on Crowley, art, music, and counterculture.
34. **Jimmy Page and Boleskine House**
    * *Description*: Historical material on the guitarist's ownership of Crowley's former estate and its later restoration efforts.
35. **Jack Parsons and the Agape Lodge**
    * *Description*: Biographical and archival resources on Crowley's American disciples and the Babalon Working.

## VII. Specialized Document Repositories

36. **Invisible Basilica of Sabazius**
    * *Description*: Essays and historical documents curated by Sabazius X°, a former National Grand Master General of the U.S. O.T.O.
37. **Agape Newsletter Archive**
    * *Description*: The U.S. Grand Lodge organ, with organizational history and essays.
38. **The Scarlet Letter**
    * *Description*: Regional O.T.O. publications that serialize letters, diaries, and historical material.
39. **Crowley Marginalia Projects**
    * *Description*: Projects dedicated to scanning and transcribing Crowley's annotated books.
40. **Boleskine House Foundation**
    * *Description*: A charitable foundation focused on restoring Crowley's former estate and providing historical context for the site.
